### Resume Analyzer
**AI-powered resume–job description matcher**

Built a multimodal NLP system to predict interview ratings from resumes and job descriptions. Combined **BERT embeddings**, **TF-IDF**, and **prosodic features** into a unified model. Optimized for semantic similarity and relevance scoring between candidate profiles and job requirements.

**Stack**: Python, PyTorch, HuggingFace Transformers, spaCy

### GeoClimb
**ML-based climbing route difficulty prediction from satellite imagery**

Developed a geospatial deep learning model that predicts climbing route difficulty using **Digital Elevation Models (DEM)** and **Sentinel-2** multispectral satellite imagery. Designed custom CNN architectures with **TorchGeo** for multi-channel satellite input; handled projection, tiling, and augmentation pipelines for geospatial data.

**Stack**: Python, PyTorch, TorchGeo, Sentinel-2, GDAL

---

### Maintenance Optimizer
**Predictive maintenance and spares planning for large-scale mining operations**

Designed and deployed a predictive maintenance system for **1,000+ machines** across Coal India's operations. Built regression tree models to forecast component failures and a spares optimization engine to minimize excess inventory. Achieved a **16.4% improvement in machine uptime** and **$50,000+ annual savings** in spare parts costs.

**Stack**: Python, scikit-learn, SQL
